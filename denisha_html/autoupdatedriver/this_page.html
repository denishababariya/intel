What Components Does Intel® Driver & Support Assistant (Intel® DSA) Detect?

Note

While Intel® Driver & Support Assistant (Intel® DSA) can detect and offer upgrades for many of the drivers and software that are available on Download Center, you may have an Intel product that isn’t supported by this tool.



Graphic drivers

The utility detects integrated and discrete Intel® Graphics Controllers for desktop and laptop computers. You can also manually identify your Intel Graphics Controller.

For all other graphics controllers, contact your computer manufacturer or place of purchase for driver support.

Wireless networking

The utility detects active Intel® Wireless Products.

It will detect discontinued products but may not offer updates for it. Contact your computer manufacturer or place of purchase for support.

Wired networking

The utility detects add-in Intel® Ethernet Adapters and Intel® Desktop Boards with integrated network connections purchased through a retailer, but will only offer updates on Intel® NUCs.

Intel sells wired networking cards to computer manufacturers who may customize the drivers. These drivers are supported only by the computer manufacturer.

Intel® Rapid Storage Technology

The utility detects updates for Intel® Rapid Storage Technology software.

Common Reasons why Intel® DSA isn't Finding your Hardware

If your hardware isn’t being detected or displayed, it may be for one of the following reasons:

  • You have an older component.
  • Your component is discontinued or isn't supported.
  • You have a component not produced by Intel.
  • The component isn't in our database.
