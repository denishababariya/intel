Intel Products That Aren’t Supported by Intel® Driver & Support Assistant (Intel® DSA)

The Intel® Driver & Support Assistant (Intel® DSA) can detect and offer updates for many of the drivers and software packages available on the Download Center. There are, however, some drivers that are not supported by this tool.

Below is a list of items that the Intel® DSA does not currently offer, but may still appear as a detected component in your system when you perform an Intel® DSA scan. To download the updates for these products, follow the links provided to Download Center.
Note

This list is subject to change.


Drivers

  • Intel server drivers
  • Linux* drivers
  • Chipset drivers
  • Intel® Graphics Media Accelerator 500 drivers
  • Intel® NUC Laptop Drivers

Other downloads

  • BIOS files(only offered for Intel® NUCs and Intel® Compute Sticks)
  • Intel® Visual Compute Accelerator software
  • Most Intel firmware(only offered for Intel® Trusted Execution Engine and Intel® SSDs)
  • Legacy (Discontinued) Intel® Wireless Adapters
